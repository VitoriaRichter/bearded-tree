After a life of adventures, Meriadoc Brandybuck, also known ans Merry, decided to settle down. He has been the owner of the Bearded Tree Tavern for 15 years, a place that he describes as his life.

The Tavern is famous for its mead, handcrafted by Merry himself and with a range of 3 different flavours - original, ginger, and lime. The Tavern is also known for its music, good food, beer and a very talented and friendly staff.

He lovingly hand-crafts each batch od his mead with care and passion and source the best natural ingredients.

The inspiration for his mead comes from all the different places he has visited and friends he has made during his life.
